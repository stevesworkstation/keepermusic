About Keeper

Hey I'm keeper besides that my real name is Tinashe and I'm a singer born in Nyanga a city in Zimbabwe and my hometown is from Rusape. I'm also born in a family of three children and in a family of five on the twenty third of May two thousand and four , which I'm the first born as well, grew up to love and listen to music and also produce my own tracks and also Albums. My educational history I went to pre-school in 2009 at Makuwaza pre-school which is from my hometown, which I attended for a week till the family relocated to Harare since my parents had to work in Harare the capital city of Zimbabwe. I then attended Primary School for grade one in 2012 which was also late since I had not got accepted at the past 2 years because there was no space at Frank Johnson Primary School but then got accepted in 2012 which we dey had to wait, since it was in search of a nearby school in the neighbourhood.I finished my grade one to grade seven Primary School in 2018 which by 2019 I started High School at Lord Malvern High School and served four years learning and finished in 2022. I started the music career in 2022 which I was just practicing and trying to find my type of genre which I liked and started with Zimbabwean Hiphop which then didn't go well for me, sadly it never got far so now I dey go with Afro which I am giving a shot now. Like I've said I grew up to love and listen to music and also produce my own tracks and also albums.
